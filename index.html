FAQ

Rates are calculated using your real engagement data plus current market benchmarks by platform and niche. They're a strong, personalised guide — consistently more accurate than any generic calculator because they factor in your specific stats.

TikTok, Instagram (Reels, feed posts, Stories), YouTube (integrations and dedicated videos), Twitter/X, and Twitch. More platforms added based on user requests.
Will brands be annoyed if I use the counter-offer email? +
No — brands expect negotiation, it's built into their process. A professional, data-backed counter-offer actually increases how seriously they take you. The email is firm and respectful, never aggressive.
Do I need a minimum follower count? +
No. NegotiRate works from 1,000 followers upward. Micro-creators often get the worst deals because they assume their rates should be low — this tool corrects that assumption with data.
