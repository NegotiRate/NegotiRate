Usage Rights on Instagram

If a brand wants to boost your post as a paid ad or use your content in their own campaigns, that's usage rights — and it should be charged separately. Many Instagram creators hand over rights for free without realising.

Usage TypeAdditional Charge
Brand's organic channels (30 days)+10 – 20%
Paid ads / boosting (30 days)+25 – 50%
Paid ads (90 days)+50 – 100%
Exclusivity (no competitor posts)+30 – 60%
📌 Always ask before you quote Before you send any rate, ask the brand: "Will you be using this content in paid advertising?" If the answer is yes, your rate needs to reflect that. Quoting without knowing is how creators undercharge.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should a US Instagram creator charge for a brand deal?
US Instagram creators with 10k–50k followers typically charge $300–$900 per post or Reel. Creators with 50k–200k followers charge $900–$3,500. Above 200k, rates start at $3,500 and scale with engagement and niche. Use the NegotiRate calculator for a personalised figure.
How much should I charge for Instagram Stories vs Reels?
Stories are typically 20–30% of your feed post rate. Reels should be 100–150% of your feed post rate. Never bundle all three at a discounted package price — price each deliverable separately.
Does engagement rate matter more than followers on Instagram?
Increasingly yes. A micro creator with 15k followers and 7% engagement often commands higher rates per post than someone with 100k followers and 1% engagement. Brands have got smarter about measuring actual impact over vanity metrics.
